Publication Ethics

We are committed to maintaining high ethical standards in research and publication. Authors, reviewers, and editors must follow these principles:

1. Originality

Submissions must be original and not previously published or submitted elsewhere.

2. Plagiarism

All manuscripts are screened for plagiarism. Content exceeding 15% plagiarism will be rejected.

3. Authorship

Only those who made significant contributions should be listed as authors.

4. Conflict of Interest

All conflicts of interest must be disclosed by authors and reviewers.
